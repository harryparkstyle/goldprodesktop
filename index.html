How to Download AOL Desktop Gold

  1. Visit the AOL official site and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to “My Benefits.”
  3. Click on the option labeled Download AOL Desktop Gold.
  4. Save the .exe file on your computer — usually, it’s saved in your Downloads folder.

How to Install AOL Desktop Gold

  1. Open your Downloads folder and double-click Install_AOL_Desktop.exe.
  2. Follow the on-screen installation wizard instructions.
  3. Once installed, launch AOL Desktop Gold and sign in.

How to Uninstall AOL Desktop Gold

  1. Open Control Panel > Programs > Uninstall a program.
  2. Select AOL Desktop Gold from the list.
  3. Click Uninstall and follow the prompts.
  4. Restart your PC to finish the process.

How to Reinstall AOL Desktop Gold

  1. Make sure the previous version is completely removed.
  2. Use Disk Cleanup to remove any temporary files.
  3. Sign in to your AOL account from a browser.
  4. Click Reinstall AOL Desktop Gold under My Benefits.
  5. Download and follow the same install steps above.
